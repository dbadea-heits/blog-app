## From the editor

### On slow reading

The web rewards velocity. We are interested in the opposite: the kind of writing that asks you to sit with it, circle back, and underline.

Every piece here was put down because someone believed it mattered enough to keep.

### On typography

We set in serif and sans with a paper-like contrast against ink.

Italics are not decoration; they are emphasis, breathing rhythm into a line.

### On the archive

Nothing here disappears. The newest issue is on the cover; prior entries wait in the reading room, numbered and intact.

Read forward or backward. Both are encouraged.
